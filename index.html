Our Philosophy

Built for the
long horizon

I seek exceedingly high-quality companies with dominant market positions, high barriers to entry, deeply entrenched brands, cash-rich balance sheets, and predictably high returns on capital — businesses that are gatekeepers, monopolies, or toll booths in their industries.

01
Quality First
I own businesses with durable competitive advantages, strong moats, and pricing power that persists through economic cycles.
02
Concentrated Conviction
A focused portfolio of my best ideas delivers superior results over diversified mediocrity. I act with conviction when the evidence is compelling.
03
Downside Protection
Preserving capital is as important as growing it. I use valuation discipline and position sizing to manage risk — not volatility metrics.
04
Time as Ally
Compounding demands patience. My minimum investment horizon is 7+ years. Short-term volatility is the price of long-term outperformance.
